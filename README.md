# Realistic Blood Effects

**HOW TO USE**

Just shoot an npc or a player and then there will not be unrealistic blood baths. 
Just bullet holes and just a tiny bit of blood on the ground to keep it more realistic.
Also the blood comes on floor on what body part you shoot. (for example if you shoot the foot near the 
ground then blood WILL come out on floor. If you shoot stomach with hollow points then there will be no blood on floor under him and only a hole)

**--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**

**HOW TO INSTALL**

Drag and drop the "realisticbloodeffects" folder into your FiveM server's resources folder. Then type "ensure realisticbloodeffects" in the server.cfg
